FAQ

How much do you charge?
Typically, we charge per hour, so it depends on the project. That being said, our projects typically range from $5000.00 - $10,000.00.
How long do your projects take?
We find most of web based projects take a minimum of 2 months to get an MVP up and running. Our mobile projects typicaly take 3-4 months to get an MVP up and running.
What if I have more questions?
You can always contact us on our contact page.
